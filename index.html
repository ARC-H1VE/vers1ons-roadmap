AI-First Development: How 7 People Ship Like 15

Vers1ons adopted an Anthropic-first, Claude Code-forward development approach from day one. Domain experts who understand music business, distribution, community, and product ship production features directly, eliminating the traditional back-and-forth between product, engineering, and domain teams. The result is role compression: a distribution specialist implements distribution features, a community manager builds community tooling, a product manager ships full-stack code. Each person carries knowledge that would otherwise require additional headcount or outside consultants.
